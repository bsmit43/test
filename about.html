Our History

test Automotive was founded in 1978 by John test with a simple vision: to provide the community with reliable vehicles and honest service. Starting as a small family-owned dealership, we quickly gained a reputation for integrity and customer satisfaction.

Over the years, we have expanded our inventory to include a wide range of new and used cars, trucks, and SUVs. We have also invested in state-of-the-art service facilities and a team of highly trained technicians to ensure that our customers receive the best possible care.

Today, test Automotive remains committed to the values that have guided us since the beginning. We are proud to be a part of the community, and we look forward to serving you for many years to come.
