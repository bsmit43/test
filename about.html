About Test Commercial Banking

Our Mission

At Test Commercial Banking, our mission is to empower businesses of all sizes to achieve their financial goals through tailored solutions, expert guidance, and unwavering commitment. We strive to be a trusted partner, fostering long-term relationships built on integrity, transparency, and mutual success.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our interactions.
  • Customer Focus: We prioritize understanding and meeting our clients' needs.
  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and continuous improvement to deliver cutting-edge solutions.

Our History

Founded on Trust and Innovation

Test Commercial Banking was founded in 1985 with a vision to provide personalized financial solutions to businesses in our community. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a leading commercial bank, serving clients across the region.

Over the years, we have adapted to the evolving financial landscape, embracing technological advancements and expanding our range of services to meet the changing needs of our clients. Our commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction has been the driving force behind our success.

  • 1985: Founding of Test Commercial Banking

    Established with a focus on local businesses.

  • 1998: Expansion of Services

    Introduced online banking and expanded loan offerings.

  • 2010: Regional Growth

    Opened new branches across the region.

  • 2023: Digital Transformation

    Launched mobile banking app and enhanced digital services.
