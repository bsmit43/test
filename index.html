Our Story

Test Automotive was founded in 1985 with a simple mission: to provide the community with reliable vehicles and honest service. What started as a small family-owned dealership has grown into a trusted name in automotive retail.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to customer satisfaction. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to helping you find the perfect vehicle to fit your needs and budget. We offer a wide selection of new and used cars, trucks, and SUVs, all thoroughly inspected and maintained to ensure your peace of mind.

At Test Automotive, we believe in building long-term relationships with our customers. We're not just selling cars; we're providing solutions and building trust. Visit us today and experience the Test Automotive difference!
